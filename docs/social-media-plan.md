# Social Media Plan

## Goal

Turn Papai's Instagram into a daily restaurant growth engine.

## Content Pillars

- Daily specials
- Behind the kitchen
- Menu item spotlight
- Team culture
- Customer moments
- Family heritage
- Reservation reminders
- Local Curaçao culture

## Weekly Rhythm

- Monday: Weekly special
- Tuesday: Kitchen behind the scenes
- Wednesday: Menu item spotlight
- Thursday: Hiring and team culture
- Friday: Weekend reservation push
- Saturday: Customer/social proof
- Sunday: Family/local story

## CTA System

Every post should point to one action:

1. Reserve by WhatsApp
2. Ask for today's special
3. Apply for work
4. Leave a review
5. Share with a friend
