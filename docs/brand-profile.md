# Papai Brand Profile

## Brand Essence

Papai Since 1933 is positioned as a local Curaçao restaurant with a heritage feel, warm service, and casual dining energy.

## Brand Tone

- Warm
- Direct
- Welcoming
- Local
- Family-oriented

## Visual Direction

- Black background
- Bold white typography
- Orange accent
- Heritage restaurant feel
- Simple, readable layouts

## Customer Promise

A welcoming local place where guests can eat, gather, reserve a table, and feel connected to Curaçao culture.

## Content Pillars

1. Daily food specials
2. Behind the kitchen
3. Staff and hiring
4. Customer moments
5. Family and heritage story
6. Reservation reminders
7. Weekend promotions
8. Local Curaçao culture
